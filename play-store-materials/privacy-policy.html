Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This privacy policy applies to the BuzzOff mobile application (“app”) and describes how we handle information in connection with the app.

Who we are

BuzzOff is a speed camera alert app. The app is offered by the operator of buzzoff.me (“we”, “us”). For privacy-related questions you can contact us via the contact details published on buzzoff.me.

What data the app uses

The app uses the following data on your device:

  • Location (precise) — To show your position on the map, detect when you are driving, and trigger alerts when you approach speed or red light cameras. Location is processed on your device and is not sent to our servers for tracking or advertising.
  • Activity recognition — To detect when you start or stop driving (e.g. in a car) so that alerts are only active when relevant. This is done on your device using the operating system’s activity APIs.

Camera data (positions of speed cameras) is downloaded as country packs from our servers when you choose a country. Once downloaded, that data is stored only on your device and used locally for alerts. We do not use it to track your location or identify you.

Where data is stored

Location and activity data are used in real time on your device and are not uploaded to us. The only data we receive in connection with the app is:

  • Requests to list available countries and to download camera packs (e.g. IP address, which country pack was requested). We may use this for operational and security purposes and to improve our service. We do not link this to your identity.

We do not require an account to use the app. We do not collect or store your name, email, or other account data through the app.

No ads or selling data

The app does not show ads. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your location or activity data for advertising or profiling.

Your choices

You can revoke location or activity permissions in your device settings at any time. Doing so will limit or disable alert and map features. You can uninstall the app at any time; data stored on your device (including downloaded camera packs) will be removed with the app.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or the app, please use the contact information on buzzoff.me.
