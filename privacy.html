Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

OVERVIEW

BuzzOff is a speed camera alert app. We believe your driving data is your business. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it's used.

The short version: Your location data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information.

DATA WE ACCESS

Location Data (GPS)

  • BuzzOff accesses your device's precise GPS location to detect nearby speed cameras and provide proximity alerts.
  • Location data is processed entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • Location access runs in the background to provide alerts while you use other apps (e.g., Waze, Spotify).

DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

BuzzOff does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Account or login credentials (no account is required)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Browsing history or app activity
  • Contacts, photos, files, or messages
  • Location history (GPS data is processed in real-time and not stored)

CAMERA DATA PACKS

  • Speed camera location packs are downloaded from our servers to your device.
  • Downloads are anonymous — no user identifiers are sent with the request.
  • Once downloaded, all camera data is stored locally on your device and used offline.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

BuzzOff does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The app uses OpenStreetMap tiles for the map display, which are loaded directly from OpenStreetMap's public servers.

INTERNET USAGE

The app uses an internet connection only for:

  • Downloading speed camera data packs (one-time per country)
  • Loading map tiles for display

No personal data is transmitted during these operations.

DATA STORAGE

All app data (camera packs, settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any user database or server-side storage of user information.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

BuzzOff is a driving utility intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
